About RahulPackaging

Delivering Excellence in Foil Printing for the Pharmaceutical Industry

Rahul packaging started with a humble beginning in 2005. Since then it has grown steadily in the printing industry mainly catering to pharmaceutical companies and sugar mills. It now has an installed capacity of printing around 350 tonnes to 600 tonnes annually with inhouse 4 printing Rotogravure machines , slitting machine, and a doctoring machine. Our quality control department has a well equipped laboratory to work and test the material on all parameters enabling us to maintain a good quality of printed foils with next to zero defects.

At Rahul Packaging, we believe in delivering excellence through advanced technology, quality assurance, and customer-centric solutions.

  • Specialized in pharmaceutical-grade aluminum foil printing.
  • Trusted by leading pharmaceutical companies across India.
  • State-of-the-art printing technology ensuring precision and quality.

Our dedication to quality and innovation makes us the preferred partner for pharmaceutical companies seeking reliable and efficient packaging solutions.

4 Ply Laminate Pouches

Packaging manufacture and export a wide range of 3Ply/4PLY Laminate Pouches. These pouches combine multiple layers to provide enhanced protection.

PVC/PVDC

Rahul Packaging provides medical-grade polyvinyl chloride (PVC) used in a wide range of applications for packaging medicines. PVC is available in thickness 0.25mm,0.35mm and PVDC coated in 40gsm, 60gsm.90gsm as requested by customer

Aluminium Blister Foil

Commonly used in pharmaceuticals for packaging tablets and capsules.Primer coated Aluminium foil, hard tempered with HSL coating is available in various thickness ranging from 20 microns to 30 microns as requested by customers.

Polyester Laminates

Polyester laminates are widely used in flexible packaging. They offer durability, moisture resistance, and Rahul Packaging offers excellent printability, provides innovative packing solutions making them suitable for a range of applications.

Polypropylene Granules

Offers high quality polypropylene granules in India, offers high-quality PP resins to fulfil your manufacturing needs. Polypropylene (PP) Granules are the building blocks for a wide range of plastic products you encounter every day.

Alu-Alu

Rahul Packaging also Supplies Alu-Alu foils used exclusively in the pharmaceutical industry for blister packaging. Alu Alu Foil is available in 130 & 150 Mic. Aluminum foil offering better mileage and cost saving to the customers.

Aluminium Foil Poly

Aluminium foil laminated with polyethylene offers a versatile packaging solution, often used in madicines and food packaging. Aluminium foil is safer and healthier than other materials for making sealing films. The surface of the aluminium foil is very clean, and any bacteria or microorganisms cannot grow on the surface of the aluminium foil.

Glassine Paper Poly

Rahul Packaging is a trusted name among Glassine Paper Suppliers;Glassine paper is a smooth and glossy paper that is often combined with polyethylene to create packaging materials suitable for items like confectionery, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.

Triple Laminate Foils

Rahul Packaging also offers packing for conservative products like confectionery, tea, consumables etc. These laminates offer a combination of strength, barrier properties, and printability.
